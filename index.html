Who We Are

Built by Industry.
Backed by Academia.

IDR exists at the intersection of academic rigour and real-world consequence.

UK University Partnership Formally aligned with leading higher education institutions to underpin our programmes with research and academic credibility.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for the digital, cyber, and technology risk landscape. We design and deliver practitioner-grade programmes that prepare individuals for substantive roles in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

Unlike conventional training providers, IDR operates at the convergence of academic insight and live industry projects. Our formal UK university partnership ensures every programme is grounded in current research, while our employer network means graduates are job-ready from day one — not just theoretically competent.

We are not a certification factory. We are a deployment engine for talent that understands risk architecturally — combining technical depth in AI governance, cyber resilience and regulatory compliance with the applied judgement demanded by the most complex operating environments.

What We Do

Three pillars.
One mission.

IDR's service model is built around a single objective: building the human and intellectual capital needed to manage digital risk in an era of unprecedented complexity and regulatory expectation.

01

Academy

Intensive bootcamps and structured programmes for students, career-changers and existing professionals. Our curriculum spans digital risk fundamentals, cyber resilience, AI safety and regulatory frameworks — delivered in partnership with UK universities for maximum academic and professional credibility.

Innovation & Incubation

Advisory Services
